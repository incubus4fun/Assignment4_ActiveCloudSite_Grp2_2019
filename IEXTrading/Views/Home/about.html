About
investagon
Whether you are new to investing or you just want to have fun with a new concept Investagon is for everyone. The idea is to pair your lifestyle with a portfolio of stocks that match your life interests. Whether you are a sport fanatic, an avid gamer or a Netflix watching binger we have a stock for you. The hypothetical investment will show you the opportunities you may have investing in stocks that go hand-in-hand with things you like to do in your everyday life. Welcome to Investagon and most of all have fun!
